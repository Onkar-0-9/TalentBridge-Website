CV Optimization Tips

  • Use Action Verbs: Start bullet points with powerful action verbs like "Led," "Developed," "Increased."
  • Quantify Achievements: Use numbers and percentages to demonstrate your impact.
  • Tailor Your Resume: Customize your resume for each job application.
  • Keep It Concise: Limit your resume to 1-2 pages with relevant information only.
  • Include Keywords: Use industry-specific keywords from job descriptions.

Interview Preparation Guide

Before the Interview
  • Research the company thoroughly
  • Review the job description
  • Prepare answers to common questions
  • Plan your outfit and route
  • Prepare questions to ask the interviewer
During the Interview
  • Arrive 10-15 minutes early
  • Maintain good eye contact
  • Use the STAR method for behavioral questions
  • Be honest and authentic
  • Show enthusiasm for the role
