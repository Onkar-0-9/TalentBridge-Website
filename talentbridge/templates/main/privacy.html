Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, upload a resume, or contact us. This may include your name, email address, phone number, resume content, and job preferences.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Match you with relevant job opportunities
  • Send you notifications about new job matches
  • Respond to your comments and questions
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve our platform

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with employers when you apply for jobs, and with service providers who assist us in operating our platform.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. You can also request a copy of your data or opt-out of certain data processing activities.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@talentbridge.com.
